About Block Net

chevron_left

True peer to peer online messaging

> Imagine being able to talk directly to your friends online without a middle man

> Block Net offers decentralised p2p messaging through the blockchain

> When you send a message, a block is added to the chain

Shared rooms for group conversations

> Chat with a bunch of your friends together in private rooms where only those invited can participate

> Like Discord (but it's cooler on blockchain)

> Private messages are actually private

> With Block Net you don't need to worry about the Zucc peering over your shoulder

> Your messages belong to you and you alone
